Privacy Policy

LiveMatch.com.ng (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website, mobile applications, and other services (collectively, the “Service”).

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that falls into two main categories: Personal Information (data you provide) and Non-Personal & Usage Data (data collected automatically).

1.1. Personal Information (Data You Provide)

This includes information that directly or indirectly identifies you:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, and phone number, typically collected when you contact us via email, sign up for a newsletter, or submit a form.
  • Correspondence: Records of communications when you interact with our support or sales team.

1.2. Non-Personal & Usage Data (Data Collected Automatically)

This data is collected automatically via cookies, pixels, and third-party tools:

  • Technical Data: Device type, browser type and version, operating system, and IP address.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, links clicked, time spent on the Service, referring website addresses, and general browsing behavior.

2. How We Use Your Information

We process your data for the following purposes:

  • **Service Operation & Improvement:** To operate, maintain, and improve our website and services.
  • **Communication:** To communicate with you and respond to your inquiries.
  • **Advertising & Personalization:** To display relevant ads and personalize your browsing experience.
  • **Analytics:** To analyze visitor behavior through tools like Google Analytics.
  • **Security:** For security and fraud prevention purposes.

3. Cookies, Google AdSense, and Third-Party Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience, analyze website performance, and serve advertisements. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your previous visits to our website or other websites.

Google uses the DoubleClick cookie and other tracking technologies to show personalised advertisements. This enables Google and its partners to:

  • Serve interest-based ads.
  • Measure ad performance.
  • Limit how often ads are shown.

For users in certain jurisdictions (e.g., the EEA/UK), we rely on your **explicit consent** to place non-essential cookies (like advertising cookies) on your device, as required by the EU User Consent Policy.

You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting the following links:

  • Google Ads Personalization Settings
  • AboutAds Interest-Based Advertising Opt-Out

You may disable or manage cookies in your browser settings at any time. However, this may affect certain features on the website.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your Personal Information. Limited non-personal information may be shared with trusted third-party partners for analytics, advertising, hosting, and website optimization purposes (e.g., Google Analytics, advertising partners). These partners operate under strict confidentiality and data-protection agreements. We may also disclose data if required by law or to protect our rights.

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect your information. Despite our efforts, no method of data transmission or online storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection. We retain your data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy.
